Who We Are

Welcome to Legends, a fansite for the game Mobile Legends: Bang bang. Legends is built by fans, and our goal is to provide top-level quality content for the community such as guides, hero information, meta tier list, and more.

About the Game

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang is a multiplayer online battle arena mobile game developed and published by Moonton. The game was first released in China for Android on July 11, 2016 and later for iOS and worldwide on November 9, 2016.
